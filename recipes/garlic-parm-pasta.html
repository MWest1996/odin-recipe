Garlic Parmesan Beef & Bowtie Pasta

Something something slogan

Ingredients

  • 1lb 93/7% Lean Ground Beef, Turkey or Chicken
  • 16 oz Cottage Cheese
  • 15 oz Pasta
  • 10 oz Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 4 oz Parmeasan
  • 6 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup Water or Milk
  • 1/2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Tsp Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Cook your pasta according to the instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, combine the cottage cheese, parmeasan, and milk/water in a food processor or blender until smooth
    • If you don't have a food processor, you can combine these by hand. The texture likely won't be as smooth as a result.
  3. Heat a pan over medium high heat, and cook the meat until not pink.
  4. Once cooked, add the cherry tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes or until the tomatoes start to wilt
  5. Add the seasoning and cook for 30 seconds, then add the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  6. Add the sauce and simmer on low for 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Combine with the pasta and use salt and pepper to taste.
